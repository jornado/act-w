Advancing the Careers of Technical Women

Building the Future of Women in Tech

The Portland tech scene is growing, adding more and more opportunities for both current and future professionals.


ACT-W is designed to help women professionals network, grow their skills, and discover employers looking for exceptional talent. The conference is organized by ChickTech, Women Who Code, and Geek Girls Carrots, with major support from sponsors CDK Global and Zulily. Dont miss this opportunity to meet tech and business mentors, learn from our speakers, build technical and social skills, and exchange experiences (and business cards).


This event is open to the public and is an exceptional forum for those who are dedicated to the advancement of women in technology. Whether entry level or executive, we welcome all professionals in the industry and invite you to take advantage of this networking event!

Sessions


Our sessions will cover useful career topics, including The Art of Negotiation, and Failure IS an Option. We will also host specific career-related topics in leadership, programming, graphic design, and more.


Our mentors, speakers, and workshop leaders are the employees and founders of leading tech companies and startups in Portland. If this sounds like you, we are accepting proposals. Teaching others is a wonderful way to inspire your community, meet other amazing people, and build your personal brand!

Call for Proposals

Are you interested in doing a lightning talk, running a hands-on workshop, leading a discussion, or speaking opportunities?

We will review your application and notify you by April 2nd if you've been chosen. Thank you for joining us in advancing the careers of women in technology!

The Organizers


Chick Tech


ChickTech is dedicated to retaining women in the technology workforce and increasing the number of women and girls pursuing technology-based careers. We facilitate hands-on technology-centric events to empower, support, and increase the confidence of women and girls. Through our events, we build community, empower participants to see themselves as leaders, and provide networking and mentoring opportunities in the rapidly growing high tech industry.


Women Who Code


Women Who Code is a non-profit dedicated to inspiring women to excel in technology careers. We provide an avenue into tech, empower women with skills needed for professional advancement, and provide environments where networking and mentorship are valued. The organization has executed more than 1,200 free events around the world, garnered a membership exceeding 25,000, and has a presence in 15 countries.

Geek Girls Carrots

Geek Girls Carrots is a global organisation focused on connecting, learning and inspiring women in Tech. We build community by organising meetings, workshops, and other events which gather people to share their knowledge and experience. Since 2011 we have built the local communities in 24 cities in Europe, US and Asia. Till 2015 weve conducted about 147 workshops in Python, Java Script, Scrum, UX. Each month over 1000 people participate in our regular meetings across all our cities.
